Italian Western Classic from 1972 starring Gordon Mitchell: Trinity in Eldorado - After several unsuccessful fraud attempts, Con man Jonathan Duke and his partner Sebastian Carter teams up with Ringo and his gang to rob the Mexican general Eldorado. 

Trinity in Eldorado (1972)
Directors: Diego Spataro (as Dick Spitfire), Joe D'Amato (uncredited)
Writers: Alberto Piferi (dialogue), Romano Scandariato (screenplay)
Stars: Stelvio Rosi, Gordon Mitchell, Luciano Conti 
Genre: Western 
Country: Italy
Language: English
Release Date: 12 November 1972 (Italy) 
Also Known As: Arriva Eldorado 

Storyline:
Stelvio Rosi is Jonathan Duke III and Gordon Mitchell is Sebastian Carter, partners and con artists roaming the West, looking for an easy buck.

When selling magic elixir and cheating at cards doesn’t yield the desired fortune, they decide to rob a rich eccentric named Eldorado (Craig Hill).

Knowing Eldorado has a band of adoring followers, Jonathan recruits Ringo Jones and his gang to help pull the job.

Knowing he’ll need a way into Eldorado’s lair, he forms an allegiance with the eccentric’s pretty niece Juanita (Daniela Giordano), promising her an even split of the gold.

But with a fortune at stake, can Jonathan and Sebastian really trust these new partners? Written by Mark Franklin on http://onceuponatimeinawestern.com
