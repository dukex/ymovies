Storyline:<br />
Stelvio Rosi is Jonathan Duke III and Gordon Mitchell is Sebastian Carter, partners and con artists roaming the West, looking for an easy buck.<br />
<br />
When selling magic elixir and cheating at cards doesn’t yield the desired fortune, they decide to rob a rich eccentric named Eldorado (Craig Hill).<br />
<br />
Knowing Eldorado has a band of adoring followers, Jonathan recruits Ringo Jones and his gang to help pull the job.<br />
<br />
Knowing he’ll need a way into Eldorado’s lair, he forms an allegiance with the eccentric’s pretty niece Juanita (Daniela Giordano), promising her an even split of the gold.<br />
