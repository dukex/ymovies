Storyline:<br />
Bandits are terrorizing Clayton City, and they just aren’t stealing money, they’ve started kidnapping leading citizens and holding them for princely ransoms.<br />
<br />
Mayor Frank Colony (Franco Ricci) pays the $15,000 ransom demanded for pretty Susy Prescott (Simonetta Vitelli), but no sooner is she freed than the bandits snatch Ricky, the young son of two other well-off residents of Clayton City.<br />
<br />
With all other plans to capture the bandits spoiled, including an ambush gone awry, Sartana is recruited to bring justice to the area.<br />
<br />
The mysterious leader of the outlaws immediately recognizes him as a threat and puts all thievery on hold until he’s eliminated.<br />
<br />
And to make sure that happens, he travels far and wide to hire four specialists, the whip-wielding Buffalo, the knife-throwing Ramirez, the champion strongman Sullivan and a fast gun named Silky.<br />
